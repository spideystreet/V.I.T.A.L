## Positioning & Features

- Long-established **HRV-based wellness and recovery** app.[web:142][web:143]
- Features:
  - HRV readings and detailed metrics.
  - Autonomic nervous system balance gauges.
  - Guided breathing and recovery protocols.
  - Targeted at athletes and high performers, with burnout prevention as a use-case.[web:142][web:143]

## Relevance vs V.I.T.A.L

- Shows how detailed HRV metrics + guided breathing can be packaged for **long-term users**.
- V.I.T.A.L focuses less on athletic performance and more on **work-related stress and burnout**.
