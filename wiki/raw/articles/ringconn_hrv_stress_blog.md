## Educational Content

- Explains in simple language that:
  - HRV reflects **balance between stress and recovery**.
  - High HRV = better recovery and adaptability.
  - Low HRV = fatigue, stress, or overtraining.[web:154]
- Emphasises **patterns and trends over single measurements**.[web:154]
