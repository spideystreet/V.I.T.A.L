## Key Findings

- Mental health indicators in France **continued to deteriorate in 2023**, especially among young adults.[web:126]
- 18–24-year-olds show the highest levels of depressive symptoms and psychological distress.[web:126]
- The article synthesises several Santé publique France monitoring waves.
