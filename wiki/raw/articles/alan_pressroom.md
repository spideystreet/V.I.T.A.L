## Alan Walk

- Step counter with rewards mechanics
- Users earn "berries" (points) redeemable for discounts in Alan Shop, charity donations, or custom avatars
- **+1,000 steps/day average increase** among active users
- Company challenges and colleague leaderboards
- **Pattern directly relevant for V.I.T.A.L: reward loop for healthy behaviors**

## Prévenir – Occupational Health Initiative (Q3 2025)

- Alan co-founded "Prévenir" with unions (CFDT, CFTC) and employers
- New inter-company Occupational Health and Prevention Service (SPSTI)
- Operational license obtained Oct. 2, 2025 for Île-de-France
- Deployment in Île-de-France starts Q1 2026
- **Focus: prevention, medical follow-up, digital care coordination for workers**
- **This is Alan's explicit bet on workplace health → directly aligned with V.I.T.A.L's burnout angle**

## Gaps vs V.I.T.A.L

- Alan Play rewards steps and mindfulness from 3rd party apps — but **no HRV/sleep/stress reading**
- Alan Walk = step-based only, no biometric context
- Mo = reactive (user asks a question) — **V.I.T.A.L is proactive (agent detects stress signals and initiates)**
- No weekly health check-up or burnout prevention ritual in current Alan offering
- **V.I.T.A.L opportunity: fill the proactive biometric coaching gap Alan hasn't built yet**
