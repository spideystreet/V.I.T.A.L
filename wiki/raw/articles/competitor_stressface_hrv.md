## Positioning & Features

- Apple Watch app that uses HRV from HealthKit to compute a **1–10 stress score in real time**.[web:147][web:153]
- Provides:
  - Stress score timeline throughout the day.
  - Guided meditations and breathing exercises.[web:147][web:153]

## Relevance vs V.I.T.A.L

- Simple pattern V.I.T.A.L can reuse:
  - Turn complex HRV into an **easy-to-understand score**.
  - Link high stress episodes with immediate micro-interventions.
- Missing vs V.I.T.A.L:
  - No weekly burn-out check-up ritual.
  - No workplace / burnout framing.
  - No deep integration with health insurer context.
