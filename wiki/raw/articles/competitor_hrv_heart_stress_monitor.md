## Positioning & Features

- Turns iPhone + Apple Watch into a **“professional health lab”** to track HRV, manage stress and **prevent burnout**.[web:144][web:151]
- Key features:
  - Daily readiness & burnout prevention score telling you when to push or rest.[web:144][web:151]
  - Integration of HR, HRV, blood pressure, sleep, workout analysis.[web:151]
  - Real-time stress monitoring, trend analysis, adaptive breathing exercises.[web:151]

## Relevance vs V.I.T.A.L

- Very close on **metrics** (HRV, HR, sleep, recovery).
- Differences:
  - V.I.T.A.L is **voice-first**, not dashboard-only.
  - V.I.T.A.L is **rooted in insurer / workplace context (Alan)** and focused on burnout at work.
  - V.I.T.A.L aims for **weekly workplace check-ups** and burnout prevention, not just generic “readiness”.
