## Core Engagement Loop Idea

Inspired by Alan Play data (10% DAU, 26% WAU) and Alan Walk reward mechanics:

**V.I.T.A.L should not be a passive dashboard — it should be a proactive health ritual system.**

### Weekly Check-Up Ritual

- Once a week, V.I.T.A.L agent initiates a voice-based health check-up
- Agent reads Apple Health data (HRV, sleep, activity, heart rate) from the past 7 days
- Generates a personalized burnout/stress risk score
- Asks 3–5 contextual voice questions to enrich biometric data with subjective state
- Produces a weekly summary: "Your nervous system recovered well Monday–Wednesday, but HRV dropped Thursday–Friday. You worked 11h both days. Let's talk about it."
- **Reward: completing the weekly check-up unlocks a badge / streak counter**

### Daily Micro-Habits (Gamified)

Inspired by Alan Walk (+1,000 steps/day via rewards) and Alan Play (turning prevention into a habit):

- **Pause reminders**: after N hours of continuous work (detected via activity / no movement), V.I.T.A.L agent suggests a break
- **Reward per pause taken**: micro-reward system (points, streaks, badges)
- **Breathing / mindfulness nudge**: agent proposes a 2-minute breathing session if HRV is low
- **Reward for weekly check-up completion**: e.g. "3-week streak → you've been monitoring your health consistently"
- **Team challenges** (if B2B / Alan context): company leaderboard for teams maintaining healthy recovery scores

### Why This Works (evidence-based)

- Alan Play proves the loop works: 10% DAU, 26% WAU, +163% wellbeing purchases
- Alan Walk proves rewards increase behavior: +1,000 steps/day
- **V.I.T.A.L extends this to the mental/stress dimension Alan hasn't covered yet**
- Biometric data (HRV, sleep) makes the coaching objective and credible, not just self-reported

### Differentiation vs Alan Play

| Feature | Alan Play | V.I.T.A.L |
|---|---|---|
| Data source | Steps, mindfulness (3rd party) | HRV, sleep, heart rate (HealthKit) |
| Coaching trigger | User-initiated | **Proactive (agent-initiated)** |
| Focus | Physical activity | **Stress, burnout, mental health** |
| Format | Gamified app | **Voice-first agent** |
| Check-up | None | **Weekly biometric ritual** |
| Workplace angle | Teams leaderboard (steps) | **Burnout prevention at work** |
