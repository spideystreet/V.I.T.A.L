## Positioning & Features

- Marketed as a **24/7 personal stress tracker, HRV tracker, and AI stress coach** for Apple Watch.[web:150]
- Uses **HRV and resting heart rate from Apple Health** to:
  - Compute current stress status.
  - Provide instant notifications if stress escalates.
  - Deliver personalised insights and coping techniques.[web:150]
- Includes a **Daily Recovery Score (0–100)** to indicate how ready you are for the day.[web:150]

## Relevance vs V.I.T.A.L

- Very similar to V.I.T.A.L on:  
  - Continuous HRV-based stress monitoring.  
  - Recovery score concept.
- V.I.T.A.L differentiators:
  - Focus explicitly on **burnout at work** (not generic stress).
  - **Weekly voice-based check-ups** and rituals.
  - Potential integration with **Alan’s ecosystem** and occupational health actors.
