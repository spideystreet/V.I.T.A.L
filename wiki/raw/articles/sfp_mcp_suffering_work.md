## Surveillance of Work-Related Diseases

- SPF monitors **musculoskeletal disorders and psychological suffering** as diseases with occupational origin.[web:117]
- Psychological suffering at work is linked to:
  - Work organisation.
  - Constraints on time and workload.
  - Relational and ethical issues (value conflicts, lack of recognition).[web:117]

## Relevance for V.I.T.A.L

- Shows psychological suffering is not just an individual weakness but recognised as **work-related disease category**.
- V.I.T.A.L can:
  - Track **individual trajectories** (stress, HRV, sleep) that might indicate progression toward these conditions.
  - Help **document context** (workload patterns, rest quality) for occupational health discussions.
