## Positioning & Features

- Marketed as a **professional stress companion** for Apple Watch.[web:156]
- Features:
  - Continuous HRV-based stress monitoring.
  - Smart alerts when stress is high.
  - Sleep analysis and personalised plans.
  - Long-term trend reports on stress, activity, sleep.[web:156]

## Relevance vs V.I.T.A.L

- Very similar on the **continuous monitoring + alerts + plans** pattern.
- V.I.T.A.L adds:
  - **Burnout at work** as explicit target.
  - Voice-based coach.
  - Link to Alan and occupational health (Prévenir, etc.).
