## Product Positioning

- AI-powered health and sport coach, centered on multi-source data
- Direct competitor to Strava, Google Fit, Apple Fitness+
- **Core proposition: centralize all health data + interpret it with a contextual AI coach**

## Integrated Data

- Physical activity (steps, workouts)
- Sleep (duration, quality, cycles)
- Heart rate and HRV
- Body temperature, SpO2
- Compatible with Apple Health (HealthKit) and other wearables

## AI Coach – How It Works

- Multi-data dashboard with synthetic scores
- **Coach memory: past health state, injuries, habits, preferences**
- Personalized recommendations adjusted over time
- Coaching for sport AND recovery AND lifestyle

## Gap Identified vs V.I.T.A.L

- Bevel = focus on sport/physical recovery
- **V.I.T.A.L = focus on stress, mental health, burnout → angle not covered by Bevel**
- V.I.T.A.L integrates voice dimension (voice-first) absent from Bevel
- V.I.T.A.L is anchored in the health insurance ecosystem (Alan) vs standalone app
