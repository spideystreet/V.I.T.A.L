## What Mo Is

- AI conversational assistant integrated in the Alan app, launched Nov. 2024
- Answers users' medical questions via chat
- Every exchange reviewed and validated by a doctor within 15 minutes
- Optional: user chooses whether to use AI or not
- Context base = Alan patient file + questions asked in chat
- **No Apple Health / HealthKit integration mentioned publicly**
- Personalization today = based on patient file, not on live biometric data

## "Health Companion" Vision

- CEO Jean-Charles Samuelian-Werve: turn Alan into a "digital AI-powered health companion"
- Mo future roadmap: understand context + health history → proactive advice
- **This is their FUTURE PLAN, not live yet → direct opportunity window for V.I.T.A.L**

## Gamification "Alan Play"

- New gamified experience announced at April keynote: Alan Play
- Vision: make health improvement as engaging as a game
- Dynamic adaptation: goal difficulty, mini-challenges, personalized challenges
- Behavioral segmentation: leaderboard for competitive profiles, streaks for solo profiles
- Behavioral learning: what the user completes/ignores → auto-adjustment

## Gap Identified vs V.I.T.A.L

- **Alan does not read real Apple Health metrics (HRV, sleep, heart rate…)**
- **V.I.T.A.L exploits this gap: real Apple Health data → contextualized stress/burnout coaching**
- Key differentiator: a health companion that ACTUALLY knows how you're doing

## Key Figures

- 680,000 members at Mo launch (Nov. 2024)
- Valuation: €5B (March 2026)
