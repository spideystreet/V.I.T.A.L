## Study Design & Results

- Used wearable ECG + HRV analysis to **assess stress in nurses at work**.[web:136]
- Found differences in HRV and stress indicators depending on:
  - Shift type (day/night).
  - Workload.
  - Unit/department characteristics.[web:136]

## Relevance for V.I.T.A.L

- Shows that **continuous HRV-based stress monitoring in real workplace conditions is feasible**.
- V.I.T.A.L can:
  - Apply similar logic to knowledge workers: track how work patterns (late-night work, long days) show up in HRV and sleep.
  - Provide **per-shift / per-day summaries** for users.
