**Reading date**: 2026-04-07

## Scope

- Review of **wearable-based approaches** to detect burnout and well-being in workers.[web:149]
- Across 10+ studies, sensors used include:
  - Heart rate and HRV.
  - Respiratory rate.
  - Skin temperature.
  - Sleep and physical activity metrics.[web:149]

## Key Conclusions

- Wearable-based detection of burnout is **feasible** but still early-stage.
- Most studies have:
  - Small sample sizes.
  - Limited external validation.
- Suggests a strong need for **larger cohorts and better algorithms**.[web:149]
