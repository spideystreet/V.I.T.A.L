## Protocol Summary

- First UK study planning **continuous HRV monitoring of physicians** to relate physiological stress to burnout.[web:141]
- Aims to link:
  - Daily HRV patterns.
  - Work schedules and workload.
  - Burnout questionnaire scores.[web:141]

## Relevance for V.I.T.A.L

- Shows how to **design a validation study** for V.I.T.A.L:
  - Continuous HRV + Apple Health data.
  - Standard burnout scales (e.g. MBI).
  - Analysis of correlations and predictive value.
