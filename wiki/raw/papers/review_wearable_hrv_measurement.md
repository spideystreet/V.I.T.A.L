## Overview

- Review of **methods and accuracy** of HRV measurement via smartwatches, rings and other wearables.[web:152]
- Discusses:
  - PPG vs ECG differences.
  - Artefact correction and data quality issues.
  - Best practices (minimal recording duration, posture control, time of day).[web:152]

## Relevance for V.I.T.A.L

- Provides guidance on:
  - When to sample HRV (morning, resting, consistent conditions).
  - How to be transparent with users about **limitations** of their device data.
- Can be used to write a wiki section “How reliable is your Apple Watch data?”.
