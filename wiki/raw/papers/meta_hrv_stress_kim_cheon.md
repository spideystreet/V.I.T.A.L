## HRV as Stress Marker

- Meta-analysis of many psychophysiological studies on stress and HRV.[web:127]
- Results show **psychological stress is reliably associated with decreased parasympathetic HRV** (especially RMSSD and HF power).[web:127]
- Stress is often accompanied by **higher heart rate and altered LF/HF ratio**, indicating autonomic imbalance.[web:127][web:134]

## Relevance for V.I.T.A.L

- Scientific backbone for using **HRV from Apple Watch as an objective stress signal**.
- V.I.T.A.L can:
  - Track low HRV episodes as potential **stress spikes**.
  - Use trends (sustained HRV reduction + poor sleep) as early markers of burnout risk.
