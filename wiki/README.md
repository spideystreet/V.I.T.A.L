# V.I.T.A.L Wiki

Research backing the [V.I.T.A.L](../README.md) burnout-prevention project — built before the Alan x Mistral hackathon (2026-04-11).

## What's in here

- **`entities/`** — partners and platforms (Alan, Mistral, Apple).
- **`concepts/`** — the science and product ideas (burnout, HRV, sleep, voice biomarkers, weekly checkup, RGPD, gamification).
- **`comparisons/`** — competitor landscape.
- **`raw/`** — source notes from articles, papers and docs.
- **`index.md`** — start here.

## Why public

V.I.T.A.L is a health AI tool pitched to a regulated insurer. The wiki is the evidence trail behind every claim made in the product and the pitch — sources, decisions, trade-offs. No code lives here.
