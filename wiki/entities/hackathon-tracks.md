# AI Health Hack 2026 — Special Tracks

"The best build in each track wins a special prize."

## Alan Play Track (4 challenges)

Gamified prevention product, 600K+ members. Berries, challenges, leaderboards, streaks.

### 1. Living Avatars
AI-generated avatars reflecting health journey. Customizable, animatable, evolving with activity. Generate collections from prompts that work across all members.

### 2. Mo Studios: AI-Generated Health Content
Pipeline: topic + member context + format → medically accurate, personalized content in minutes. Guided meditations, articles, video scripts. Personalized by member profile.

### 3. Personalized Wrapped
Spotify Wrapped for health. Weekly/monthly recap: raw data → personal narrative + visual cards. Individual + team + league wraps. Shareable.

### 4. Health App in a Prompt
Describe a health goal → AI assembles a micro-app from phone primitives (camera, HealthKit, GPS, calendar, berries, social). Lovable-for-health.

## Alan Precision Track (3 challenges)

Deep blood panels + lifestyle data → science-backed health trajectory. Reactive → adaptive coaching.

### 1. Wearables + Blood → Daily Coaching ← OUR TARGET
- Blood panel results + daily wearable data → personalized daily protocol
- Protocol adapts each morning based on last night's data (HRV, sleep, steps, resting HR)
- Crossed with blood biomarkers (ferritin, cortisol, vitamin D, glucose)
- Show how protocol evolves over a week
- **Bonus:** flag when biomarker trend + wearable signal together catch something neither would alone

### 2. AI-Powered Medical Intake
Replace 200+ question static form with adaptive AI conversation. Multiple specialist agents (sleep, nutrition, cardio) hand off mid-conversation. Two profiles → two different conversation paths.

### 3. Agentic Health Protocols
Turn recommendations into actions an agent executes: grocery list, recipes, appointments. "Open Claw for health."
